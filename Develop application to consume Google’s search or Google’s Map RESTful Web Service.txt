AIM :- Develop application to consume Google’s search / Google’s Map RESTful Web
service. 

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Step 1 :- Search “google cloud console” in any browser, login to your gmail account and Click on console.

Step 2 :- In select project tab, Click on new project.

Step 3 :- Give a project name and then click on create.

Step 4 :- Project Dashboard will appear.

Step 5 :- In left panel, in APIs and services, Click on Enabled APIs and services.

Step 6 :- Click on + ENABLE APIS AND SERVICES.

Step 7 :- Search for ‘Custom Search Api’.

Step 8 :- Click on Custom Search Api.

Step 9 :- Click on Enable and then Try this Api.

Step 10 :- Click on control panel.

Step 11 :- Give a Search engine name, then provide a site url "www.google.com" and then click on Add.

Step 12 :- Click on I’m not a robot and then click on create.

Step 13 :- Copy the generated code.

Step 14 :- Open VS code editor and paste the code in HTML file and then Run it by Click on GO Live.

Step 15 :- Consume Google Search.
